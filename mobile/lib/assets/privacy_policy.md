# CareCircle Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

CareCircle ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

- **Account Information**: Name, email, phone number, and profile picture
- **Health Data**: Health metrics, medication schedules, and check-in responses
- **Usage Information**: How you interact with our App
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and unique identifiers

### 2.2 Health Information

Our App collects sensitive health information to provide personalized care services. This may include:

- Medical conditions
- Medication schedules and adherence
- Daily health metrics and check-ins
- Emergency contact information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain our services
- Send notifications about medications and health check-ins
- Share information with your care group as authorized
- Generate insights and recommendations about your health
- Improve our App and develop new features

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

- **Care Group Members**: Information shared according to your permissions
- **Healthcare Providers**: With your explicit consent
- **Service Providers**: Who help us deliver our services
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Export your data
- Restrict certain processing
- Withdraw consent

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We may retain certain information to comply with legal obligations or prevent fraud.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the App and, where appropriate, via email.
